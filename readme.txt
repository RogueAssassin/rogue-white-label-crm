A white-label CRM for WordPress with customers, jobs, dashboard, roles, exports, and configurable business branding.

== Description ==

Rogue White Label CRM gives a WordPress site a simple business CRM that can be branded for different businesses.

Features include customer management, job management, configurable statuses, CSV exports, CRM roles, dashboard cards, and settings for business details and branding.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin ZIP through Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin.
2. Activate the plugin.
3. Open Rogue CRM > Settings.
4. Configure business details, job statuses, and job prefix.
